About us

ITALCOL offers you the best quality construction materials in the Albanian market. They are certified under ISO 9001:2008 and European standards EN 998, EN 12004.

Our 10 years of experience have taught us to correctly fulfill all customer requirements, and this is confirmed by the positive feedback of our customers. It is important to us that our decisions are directed at improving our service to you. In this journey we will seek to increase transparency, to provide information in due time and fully meet your needs.

  • Production
  • Sales

Production

ITALCOL construction materials are dry, pre-mixed materials. To guarantee their quality, special care is given to handling the raw materials, and strict following of mixing chemical formulas. Raw materials include local Gray Portland cement (42.5R), imported White Portland cement (52.5R), as well as chemical additives of known brands such as Ashland and Wacker.

Sales

The showroom in our factory premises is used to display building materials and related information to ITALCOL trademark, but it also serves as a mini-information and training center, where we will be conducting informative workshops with construction specialists. Showroom is open from Monday to Friday, from 10:00 until 15:30.
