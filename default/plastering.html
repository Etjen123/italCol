ITC GLET

A white cement-based fine finish, used to coat concrete layers or used as a second layer on traditional finish with a maximum thickness of 2 mm. The end result of the wall or ceiling will be smooth and very white, so it may not need painting. ITC GLET may be used in indoor premises which are dry and free of moisture.

ITC 50: Dry mortar

ITC 50 is a dry mortar composed of hydrated lime, Portland cement, selected aggregates and chemical additives that improve workability and adhesion. The material is used as first coat material for plastering internal and external surfaces. It may be applied using a plastering pump or manually.

ITC 75: Hydroisolation mortar

ITC 75 is a dry, pre-mixed material used in walls and ceilings including basement works and surfaces where there may be water or moisture. It is a cement and lime based material, which when mixed with water creates a waterproof mortar. The material is for horizontal or vertical use, inside or outside and for surfaces with moisture.

ITC 125: Skim coat grey

IITC 125 is a dry mortar finish, composed of hydrated lime, Gray Portland cement, selected aggregates and chemical additives that improve workability and adhesion. This material is mainly used in interior and exterior premises as second layer of wall coating. It should not be applied on gypsum, wood, metal or previously painted surface.

ITC 175: skim coat white

ITC 175 is a dry mortar finish, composed of hydrated lime, White Portland cement, selected aggregates and chemical additives that improve workability and adhesion.
